# Conversion Landing Page Prompt

You are an expert B2B landing page copywriter. Generate a high-converting landing page based on the following product context and campaign objectives.

## Context to Include
- Reference relevant messaging from `/00-overview/brand-positioning.md`
- Use positioning from `/01-segment-context/[segment]/narrative-and-positioning.md`
- Incorporate proof points and success stories from appropriate vertical/regional contexts

## Landing Page Structure

### Hero Section
- **Headline**: [Clear, benefit-driven statement - 10 words or less]
- **Subheadline**: [Expand on the value - 15-20 words]
- **CTA**: [Primary action - button text]
- **Hero image/video**: [Description of visual]

### Problem/Solution
- **Problem agitation**: [2-3 sentences describing the pain]
- **Solution introduction**: [How your product solves it]

### Key Benefits (3-4)
For each benefit:
- Icon/visual
- Headline (3-5 words)
- Description (1-2 sentences)

### Social Proof
- Customer logos
- Testimonial quote with attribution
- Key stat or metric

### Feature Highlights
- 3-5 key features with brief descriptions
- Screenshots or product images

### Trust Builders
- Security/compliance badges
- Awards or recognition
- Integration logos

### Final CTA
- Headline restating value
- CTA button
- Optional: Risk reducer (free trial, no credit card, etc.)

## Requirements
- Keep copy concise and scannable
- Use benefit-driven language, not feature lists
- Include specific numbers and metrics where possible
- Match brand voice and tone
- Optimize for conversion, not education
- Mobile-first thinking

## Output Format
Provide full copy for each section with [NOTES] for design/imagery guidance.
