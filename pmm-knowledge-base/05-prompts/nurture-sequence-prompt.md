## Content Journey

### Email 1: Awareness/Education
Focus: Introduce problem and position yourself as helpful resource

### Email 2: Deep Dive
Focus: Educate on specific aspect of problem or solution

### Email 3: Social Proof
Focus: Show how others have solved this problem (case study/testimonial)

### Email 4: Product Introduction
Focus: Introduce your solution in context of their needs

### Email 5: Clear CTA
Focus: Drive specific action (demo, trial, purchase)

## Requirements
- Each email should stand alone (some won't read all)
- Progressive value delivery (don't sell too early)
- Varied content formats (text, links to videos, downloads, etc.)
- Match brand voice and tone
- Mobile-optimized copy (short paragraphs, scannable)
- Clear, single primary CTA per email

## Output Format
Provide complete copy for all emails with subject lines, preview text, body, and CTAs ready for ESP upload.
