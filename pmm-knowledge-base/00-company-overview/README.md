# Overview

This directory contains high-level company, product, and brand information that applies across all segments, verticals, and regions.

## Files

- `company-overview.md` - Company mission, vision, history, and positioning
- `product-portfolio.md` - Overview of your product suite and how products relate to each other
- `brand-positioning.md` - Core brand values, voice, and positioning

## Purpose

The overview section serves as the foundation for all other marketing content. It defines:
- Who you are as a company
- What you build and sell
- How you position your brand in the market

All segment, vertical, and regional messaging should align with these core principles.
