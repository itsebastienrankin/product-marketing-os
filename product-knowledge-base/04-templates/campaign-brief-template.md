#### Option 1: Manual Completion
1. **Replace all bracketed text** with your specific campaign information.

3. **Be specific with metrics** – include actual numbers and targets.  
4. **Link to resources** – add working links to Vault, workbooks, and creative assets.  
5. **Update RACI matrix** – ensure all stakeholders are properly assigned.

#### Option 2: AI-Assisted Completion (Recommended)
1. **Provide comprehensive context** including:  
   - Campaign background and objectives  
   - Target audience details and segmentation  
   - Product/service features and benefits  
   - Available data claims and metrics  
   - Customer success stories and case studies  
   - Channel strategy and asset requirements  
   - Timeline and milestones  
2. **Ask AI to fill in the template** with your specific information.  
3. **Review and refine** the AI-generated content.  
4. **Validate all links and references** before finalizing.

### Key Sections to Customize
- **Campaign Overview:** Make this the TL;DR for anyone reading the brief.  
- **Messaging Table:** This is the most important section for creative teams.  
- **Data Claims:** Include only metrics that directly support your value proposition.  
- **Social Proof:** Use real customer success stories with specific outcomes.  
- **Workstreams:** Ensure all stakeholders are listed with clear responsibilities.

---

### Tips for Success
- Keep messaging concise but specific.  
- Include both qualitative and quantitative proof points.  
- Make sure all links work and are accessible to the team.  
- Update status regularly as the campaign progresses.
