Hi {first_name},

We're launching Notion AI 2.0, and we wanted to give Enterprise admins a heads-up on what's changing and what controls you have.

**What's new:**
- Enhanced AI Search across your workspace
- AI-powered database autofill
- AI Connectors for external tools (Slack, Google Drive, GitHub)
- Improved AI writing assistance

**What hasn't changed (the important stuff for IT):**
- All AI features respect your existing permission model — users can only access content they already have access to

- Granular AI feature toggles (enable search but disable generation, for example)
- AI usage dashboard (see adoption and usage patterns)
- AI Connector approval workflow (IT approves which external tools can be connected)
- Data boundary controls for AI processing
